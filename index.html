Research Interests

In the field of Computer Vision, my interest mainly revolves around detecting, tracking and re-identifying humans (as in surveillance).

Action Recogniton, Detection & Summarization

Multimodal Learning (Image, NLP, Audio)

It is particularly interesting for me to understand, how we, as humans, are using Multimodal knowledge from the world and in the same way, will we be able to make a machine to learn using multimodal data?
